Episode 37: Now with More Chaps and Rage

16 December 2015 on Pageant, Heighway, Pinball, Film, Safe, Cracker

It’s a pre-christmas romp in the land of small wheels. Yep, we take a less structured approach to this show and let it take us where we want.

There’s an extra long, super-sized rant from Chris about School Christmas pageants but don’t be put off by that. If you are a parent, you’ll love this segment. And if you’re not, you’ll feel the pain we expereince as parents.

There is actual pin talk as well, so you’re covered. Get on it!

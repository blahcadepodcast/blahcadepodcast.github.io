Episode 58: Virtual Pinball For Real, or VPCabs Interview.

In this episode, we talk with Brad Baker from VPCabs.

Brad tells us about his experience pitching his Virtual Pinball cabinet product to The Sharks on ABC’s Shark Tank (USA) program.

It’s an engaging interview we’re sure you’ll like as a pinhead. The journey from how VPCabs started to it’s success on Shark Tank is a great story, and Brad tells it well.

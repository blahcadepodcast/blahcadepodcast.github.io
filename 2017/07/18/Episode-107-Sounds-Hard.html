Episode 107: Sounds Hard

We dive into the newly released beta on Android for Black Knight (emulated!) and Firepower 2. There’s a few tweety clues floating around about potential upcoming digital releases from Stern, and Zen for Android. There’s also some insight into how much effort is put into capturing the sounds from the Pinbal Arcade tables: a true labour of love.
